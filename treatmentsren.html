CHEMICAL PEEL


A chemical peel is a cosmetic procedure that involves the controlled application of an acid-based solution to the skin, with the goal of removing the damaged surface layer and promoting the growth of healthy new skin cells. The exfoliating agent in the solution causes controlled damage, which triggers a healing response in the skin, resulting in improved texture, appearance, and overall health.


This procedure is often used to reduce the appearance of wrinkles, pigmentary disorders, roughness, and scars. There are different types of chemical peels available, ranging from superficial to deep, depending on the desired level of skin penetration and the severity of the skin concerns being treated.
Overall, chemical peels are a safe and effective way to achieve a smoother, more youthful complexion.

MICRONEEDLING


Microneedling is a minimally invasive cosmetic procedure that involves using a needle-based device to create controlled micro-wounds on the skin's surface, which triggers a healing response that leads to improved growth and reorganization of skin cells. By stimulating the fibroblasts, the procedure enhances collagen and elastin production, resulting in improved skin texture, firmness, and elasticity. It can also help with hair growth, pigmentation, scarring, enlarged pores, and product penetration.


Microneedling is often used to address a wide range of skin concerns, including acne scars, post-traumatic and burn scars, alopecia, hyperhidrosis, stretch marks, and skin rejuvenation. However, it is important to note that post-procedure compliance is crucial to avoid complications. Therefore, patients must follow their dermatologist's post-procedure instructions diligently to achieve optimal results and avoid any potential risks.

LAMELLE PROFESSIONAL FACIALS


Lamelle professional facials are a type of skin treatment designed to improve the health and appearance of the skin. These facials use high-quality products and advanced techniques to address specific skin concerns, such as acne, hyperpigmentation, and signs of aging. They typically involve a deep cleanse, exfoliation, and the application of various serums and masks to nourish and hydrate the skin. Lamelle professional facials are tailored to each individual's unique skin type and concerns, so they can provide targeted results. The treatment is non-invasive and can leave the skin feeling refreshed, rejuvenated, and glowing.
