About Renewal


At Renewal Aesthetics by Kalafhi, our focus is on providing individualized aesthetic treatments that promote rejuvenation of the skin, body, and overall health based on proven methods. Our aim is to offer a haven where clients can revitalize their mind, body, and spirit, believing that beauty and wellness are intertwined.


We strive to provide a holistic experience that nurtures both physical and mental well-being, with treatments carefully crafted to enhance your physical appearance while leaving you feeling reinvigorated and revitalized. Our goal is to assist individuals in improving their physical appearance, uncovering their true potential, and attaining a superior quality of life.


Whether you are looking to address a specific skin concern, improve your overall well-being, or simply enhance your natural beauty, we are committed to providing you with the personalized attention and care you deserve.


Contact us today to schedule a consultation and discover how we can help you look and feel your best.

Dr. Matlhogonolo Mongwa-Mouwane

Managing Director and Esthetician

Dr Mongwa-Mouwane is a medical professional with 9 years of experience in the health sector.

In starting a business five years ago (2018), she became so consumed with “making it” which led to a neglected overall well-being. She was overworked, overwhelmed and unhappy. This led her to think of ways to combat her struggles and create an environment in which all her needs were being met. It was during this journey of healing and self-discovery that she learned of the importance of taking care of oneself and was inspired to create Renewal Aesthetics.

Her lifelong mission to transform Botswana’s healthcare and wellness system to bring it to par with some of the world’s best healthcare and wellness systems remains on the forefront of her mind as she tackles and incorporates issues faced personally to expand and aid those who face similar issues.

Dr Mongwa-Mouwane is also a Founding Director and Practicing Doctor at Kalafhi Medical Centre. A personal word - “I am incredibly proud of what Renewal represents and the impact it has had on our clients’ lives. Thank you for allowing me to share my story and the vision behind Renewal with you.”

Dr. Mongwa-Mouwane is a believer in the benefits of a holistic approach to skincare. She understands that beautiful skin comes from within and that taking care of one's mental and emotional well-being is just as crucial as using high-quality skincare products.

Dr. Mongwa-Mouwane's story serves as a reminder that success shouldn't come at the cost of our well-being, and that nobody is immune to the dangers of overworking and neglecting self-care.

Through Renewal Aesthetics and Kalafhi Medical Centre, Dr. Mongwa-Mouwane is paving the way for a new era of healthcare that puts the individual's well-being at the center of everything. She has ensured that clients can expect a personalized approach to their medical and skincare needs.

Dr Mongwa-Mouwane's experience highlights the need for more holistic approaches to healthcare, where mental and emotional well-being are given just as much attention as physical health. Her commitment to transforming Botswana's healthcare system is not just about providing medical treatments, but also promoting a culture of self-care and wellness.

"Success is not just about what you accomplish in your life, it's about what you inspire others to do. And in order to inspire, you must take care of yourself first. Nourish your mind, body, and soul so you can be the best version of yourself for not only your own success but for those around you as well."

- Unknown
