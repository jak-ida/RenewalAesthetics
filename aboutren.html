About Renewal

Welcome to Renewal Aesthetics, a luxury medical spa & clinic dedicated to helping you become the best version of yourself. Our mission is to provide top-notch self-care services and treatments that enhance and restore your physical appearance, as well as your overall health and wellbeing.

At Renewal Aesthetics, we understand that self-care is not just a luxury but a necessity in today's fast-paced world. That's why we offer a range of services, including facials, body treatments, injectables, and more, all designed to help you look and feel your best.



In addition to our traditional services, we also offer exclusive self-care luncheons and retreats that provide the ultimate luxury indulgence. Our luncheons and retreats are designed to provide a full sensory experience, from the ambiance and welcome rituals to the exquisite culinary experiences. We believe that true self-care should encompass both the mind and the body, and our self-care retreats are designed to provide you with the ultimate luxury indulgence.

Our experienced and knowledgeable staff is dedicated to providing you with the highest level of care and attention, ensuring that your experience at Renewal Aesthetics is nothing short of exceptional.

We believe that true beauty comes from within, and our goal is to help you cultivate a sense of inner peace and confidence that radiates outward. Whether you're looking for a quick pick-me-up or a full day of pampering, we invite you to experience the Renewal Aesthetics difference.

Our Services

At Renewal Aesthetics, we offer a wide range of services to help you achieve your desired look and feel your best. Our services include:

Chemical Peels: A chemical peel is a skin-resurfacing procedure that uses a chemical solution to remove the outer layers of damaged skin, revealing smoother, more radiant skin underneath.

Microneedling: Microneedling is a minimally invasive skin rejuvenation treatment that uses tiny needles to create controlled micro-injuries in the skin, stimulating the body's natural healing process and collagen production.

Medical Grade Facials: Our medical grade facials are designed to target specific skin concerns and deliver real results. We use only the highest quality products and techniques to help you achieve healthier, more youthful-looking skin.

Hair Restoration: Our hair restoration services are designed to help you regain confidence and restore your natural hairline. We offer both surgical a variety of options to meet your individual needs.

Anti-Aging Treatments: We offer a variety of anti-aging treatments, including anti-wrinkle injections, fillers, vampire facials and many more to help you look and feel younger.

Sexual Rejuvenation: Our sexual rejuvenation services are designed to improve sexual function, enhance pleasure, and boost confidence. We offer a variety of options to help you achieve your goals.

Body Contouring: Our body contouring services are designed to sculpt and tone your body, helping you achieve the look you desire.

Cosmetic Surgeries: Through partnerships with leading plastic surgeons, we are able to offer a range of cosmetic surgeries to help you achieve your desired look.

Dr. Matlhogonolo Mongwa-Mouwane

Managing Director and Esthetician

Dr. Mongwa-Mouwane, a medical professional with nine years of experience, embarked on a journey of self-discovery and healing after neglecting her own well-being while starting her business in 2018. Through this experience, she learned the importance of self-care and was inspired to create Renewal Aesthetics, a medical spa that prioritizes holistic approaches to beauty and wellness.

Driven by her lifelong mission to transform Botswana’s healthcare and wellness system, Dr. Mongwa-Mouwane strives to promote a culture of self-care and wellness. Her story serves as a powerful reminder that success should not come at the cost of our well-being and that taking care of our mental and emotional health is just as important as physical health.

Through Renewal Aesthetics and Kalafhi Medical Centre, Dr. Mongwa-Mouwane is paving the way for a new era of healthcare that puts individuals' well-being at the center of everything. Clients can expect a personalized approach to their medical and skincare needs, ensuring that they receive the highest quality care.
